BloodSource: Predict Blood Donations

We, at BloodSource aim to connect hospitals with their nearby blood banks so that hospitals would not run out of their blood reserves. Moreover, registered hospitals can quantitatively and qualitatively analyse the stored data (of blood donors) of all blood banks without any breach of blood-donor's personal information and also predict future donations with the help of same.

OUR GOALS


Increase the effectiveness and efficiency of blood donations
Analyse patterns in blood donations and make important predictions about future blood donations
Connect blood banks with hospitals
